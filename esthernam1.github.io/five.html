ESTHER NAM
5 x 5
Daily web sketches based on constraints
Sketches are artistic expressions not intended as finished works. Sketching serves many purposes—capturing and communicating ideas, exploring form, taking visual notes, developing skills and techniques—and a practice of frequent sketching is extremely valuable to artists and designers. For five consecutive days, I developed a coherent set of constraints and created a body of five web-page sketches based on those constraints.
Constraints
⟶The total of your receipt is the size of your canvas(converted to in, px, or cm).


⟶The first person you talk to, the first letter their name begins with has to be revolving around the theme/content of the sketches.


⟶The place you get your receipt from must be the color palette of your sketches(logos and brands).
